## Motivation

Modern diffusion models are powerful priors over images, signals, and scientific fields — but most posterior-sampling methods assume Gaussian observation noise. Many real measurement processes are far from Gaussian.

## Contribution

Methods that extend diffusion-based posterior sampling to general likelihoods, with applications across scientific inverse problems.

## Why it matters

Bridges the gap between expressive generative priors and the messy noise models of real-world measurement.
