## Motivation

Survival models are used to estimate event times — death, relapse, machine failure — under censoring. In clinical settings, point estimates are dangerous: doctors need calibrated uncertainty.

## Contribution

NeuralSurv introduces a Bayesian deep survival model that produces well-calibrated posterior survival curves, scaling to modern neural architectures while preserving uncertainty over both parameters and predictions.

## Why it matters

Reliable uncertainty estimates allow downstream decisions — treatment selection, follow-up scheduling, risk stratification — to be made with awareness of model confidence.
