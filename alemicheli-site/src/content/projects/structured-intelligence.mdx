## Direction

Real data is rarely flat. It lives on manifolds, graphs, hierarchies, and physical state spaces. Treating that structure as a nuisance throws away the signal that makes generalisation possible. Structured intelligence is about learning methods that respect — and exploit — this underlying geometry and probability.

## What I build

- Probabilistic deep learning that scales while remaining mathematically grounded.
- Models that operate on graphs, manifolds, and structured domains.
- Representation learning that preserves geometric and physical invariants.

## Why it matters

Structure-aware learning is often the difference between models that overfit a benchmark and models that generalise to the next dataset, the next instrument, the next decision.
