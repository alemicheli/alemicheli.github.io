## Motivation

Many real datasets live on manifolds, graphs, or other structured spaces. Treating them as flat vectors throws away the geometry that makes them informative.

## Contribution

Probabilistic models that respect the underlying geometry while remaining tractable to train and sample from.

## Why it matters

Structure-aware uncertainty is a prerequisite for trustworthy ML on scientific and decision-critical data.
